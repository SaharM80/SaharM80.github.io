About

I’ve always been curious about how things work, especially when it comes to technology and data. That curiosity led me to explore machine learning and AI, where I enjoy turning ideas into real, working models. Whether I’m training a model to understand language or building tools that simplify tasks, I like working on problems that matter.

I started out as a software developer but quickly realized my passion lay in machine learning. Since then, I’ve worked as a machine learning engineer on projects ranging from computer vision to NLP, and I’m currently deepening my skills through a postgraduate program in Applied AI Solutions Development.

I enjoy building practical AI tools that solve real-world problems—whether it's training a model to understand text or automating a complex task. In my spare time, I like keeping up with the latest tools and trends in AI and playing strategy board games.
